
# Week Two: Lesson 2.1.10

Lesson URL: https://learning.pariyatti.org/mod/page/view.php?id=306

--

1. "Sāvatthinidānaṃ"

Sāvatthi: The city of Savatthi
शवस्त्रि (Shavastri)

Nidāna: Source, reason, cause, origin, foundation ("by means of")

--

2. "Sāmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sāmaññatthañca."

Sāmaññañca => Sāmañña => samaṇa
-añca: ? not sure what this suffix means
Sāmañña: striving for samana-ship, in accordance with true samana-ship
samana: a monk, recluse, meditator

vo: to you, of you, by you (emphasis)

bhikkhave: monks

desessāmi (deseti): to point out, preach, teach, expound

sāmaññatthañca: -atthanca ? not sure what this suffix means ("purpose"?)

--

3. "Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca kho, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṃ?"

Taṃ: that ("therefore"?)

suṇātha => Suṇāti: hears (listen)

Katamañca: which, what

kho: emphatic

bhikkhave: monks

sāmaññaṃ: recluse-ship

--

4. "Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ– sammādiṭṭhi ...pe... sammāsamādhi."

ayameva => ayam: "before our eyes", "the present", "this here", "just this"

ariyo atthangiko maggo: noble eight-limbed path

Sammādiṭṭhi:    right view
sammāsaṅkappo: right thoughts, intention
sammāvāco:     right speech
sammākammanto: right action
sammā-ājīvo:   right livelihood
sammāvāyāmo:   right effort
sammāsati:     right awareness, mindfulness
sammāsamādhi:  right concentration++

--

5. "Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṃ."

Idaṃ => ida: this thing

vuccati: what is said
vutta => vatti: literally "existed", "happened"
वक्ति / वच: to speak, say call (usage here)

bhikkhave: monks

sāmaññaṃ: recluse-ship

--

6. "Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho?"

katamo => katama: which, what

ca => then, now, "how is it?", "what about?"

bhikkhave: monks

sāmaññattho: purpose of recluse-ship

--

7. "Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo."

yo => ya: any person, whoever, "something belonging to this category"

kho: indeed, surely (emphasis)

bhikkhave: monks

khayo => khaya: waste, destruction, decay, consummation of

raga: craving
dosa: aversion
moha: ignorance

--

8. "ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho."

ayaṃ: "this right here"

vuccati: "to be said", "to be called"

bhikkhave: monks

sāmaññattho: the purpose of recluse-ship
