--

0. Buddhāna sāsanaṃ

Buddhāna - genitive plural of "buddha"
sāsanaṃ - teaching; order; message; doctrine; a letter

--

1. "Sabbapāpassa akaraṇaṃ"

sabba: all; every; whole; entire
pāpassa: unwholesome, sinful actions (dative singular case)
pāpa: crime, evil action

akaraṇaṃ: a-kara
kara (karoti): does; acts
a-kara: not to do; not to act (abstain from acting)

--

2. "kusalassa upasampadā"

kusala (+assa): good action; merit; virtue (dative singular)

upasampadā: higher ordination of a monk
- generally, "undertaking"

--

3. "sacittapariyodapanaṃ"

sacitta: one's own mind
pariyodapa (pariyodapeti): cleanses; purifies

--

4. "etaṃ buddhāna sāsanaṃ"

etaṃ: this
buddhāna: plural of "buddha" (all?)
sāsanaṃ: teaching, doctrine
