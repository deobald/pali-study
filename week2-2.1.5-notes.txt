
# Week Two: Lesson 2.1.5

Lesson URL: https://learning.pariyatti.org/mod/page/view.php?id=301

1. "Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo."

dasahi: ten (`hi`: instrumental, plural)
dasa: 10 but auspicious, more meaningful than the number alone
दस - ten

dhammehi: personal qualities ("states" in lesson translation)
dhamma: quality (in this case)
धर्म

sammannagato:
Samannāgata: endowed with, possessed of (adj.)
संपन्न - endowed

puggalo:
puggala: individual / person (m.)
व्यक्ति - person, man

na: negative, no, not

sevitabbo: associate with (`bbo`: future passive participal)
sevati (sev): to serve, associate with

--

2. "Katamehi dasahi?"

katamehi: which (`ehi`: instrumental, male, plural)
katama: which / which one (of many)

dasahi: ten

--

3. "Micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, micchāsaṅkappo hoti, micchāvāco hoti, micchākammanto hoti,
micchā-ājīvo hoti, micchāvāyāmo hoti, micchāsati hoti, micchāsamādhi hoti, micchāñāṇī
hoti, micchāvimutti hoti -"

Micchā (adv.): untruth, wrongly, in a wrong way, wrong / false
Hoti (bhavati): to be, exists (in this context: "one who is")
"myth"
मिथ्या (mithya)

micchaditthiko: (`ko`: posessive adjective, applying to person)
ditthi: views
दृष्टि (drishti): view, vision

micchasankappo:
sankappa: aspiration (thought)
संकल्प (sankalp): determination

micchavaco:
vaca: speech
वाक् (vak)

micchakammanto:
kammanta (kamma + anta): final/ending action
कर्मानता (karmanta): duty

miccha-ajivo:
ajiva: livelihood
आजीविका (ajivika)

micchavayamo:
vayama: effort
प्रयास (?)

micchasati:
sati: awareness, mindfulness

micchasamadhi:
samadhi: concentration++
समाधि (samadhi)

micchāñāṇī:
āñāṇī: ...knowledge? somehow?
nyana: "to know"

micchāvimutti:
vimutti: release, deliverance, emancipation
मुक्ति (mukti): liberation

--

4. "- imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo."

imehi:
ayaṃ: this person

kho: indeed, surely (emphatic)

sam + annāgatatta (anna):

"surely this person, bhikkus, who is a person possessed of these ten qualities, must
not be served."

sevitabbo: not exactly "associate" or "serve" but to follow, to follow their views/traits

--

5. "Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo."

dasahi / dasa: ten
bhikkhave: monks
dhammehi / dhamma: qualities
samannagato / samannāgata: endowed with
puggalo / paggala: person
sevitabbo / sevati: serve, associate with

--

6. "Katamehi dasahi?"

katamehi / katama: which, which one
dasahi / dasa: ten

--

7. "Sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo hoti, sammāvāco hoti, sammākammanto hoti,
sammā-ājīvo hoti, sammāvāyāmo hoti, sammāsati hoti, sammāsamādhi hoti, sammāñāṇī
hoti, sammāvimutti hoti -"

sammaditthiko / ditthi:     right view
sammasankappo / sankappa:   right thought (aspiration)
sammavaco / vaca:           right speech
sammakammanto / kamma+anta: right (final) action
samma-ajivo / ajiva:        right livelihood
sammavayamo / vayama:       right effort
sammasati / sati:           right awareness (mindfulness)
sammasamadhi / samadhi:     right concentration
sammanani / [anani?]:       right [knowledge?]
sammavimutti / vimutti      right liberation

--

8. "imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo”ti"

imehi / ayaṃ: this person
bhikkhave: bhikkus
dasahi: ten
dhammehi: qualities
samannagato: possessed by
puggalo: an individual
sevitabbo: to serve
ti: end quote.

--
