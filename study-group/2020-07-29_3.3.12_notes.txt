
piyatarasuttam (KN 3.41)

piya: dear; amiable; beloved. (m.) the husband. (nt.) a dear thing. (adj.)
tara: (in cpds.), crossing; passing over. (adj.)

> Evaṃ me sutaṃ:
- Thus it was heard by me

> Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati
> jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

one time the bhagava was staying at/near savatthi (f.loc.)
in anathapindika's pleasure (arame) park/grove (jetavana)

> Tena kho pana samayena

tena - on account of it
pana - and now, moreover
samayena - time (ins. "at that time")

> rājā pasenadi kosalo mallikāya deviyā saddhiṃ

- king pasendati of kosala, with queen mallika

saddhiṃ - with

>  uparipāsādavaragato hoti.

upari: above; on; upon; upper; overhead. (ind.)
pāsāda: a mansion; place; castle. (m.)
??? vara: excellent; noble. (m.), a boon; favour. (adj.)
gato: to go

> Atha kho rājā pasenadi kosalo mallikaṃ deviṃ etadavoca:

- then king pasendati of kosala [said to] queen malika:

> “Atthi nu kho te, mallike, kocañño attanā piyataro”ti?

atthi (3rd. sing.): to be, to exist
nu: interrogative particle
te => tuvaṃ: thou. (nom. sin. of tumha)
kocañño: koc (saṃ? "some one"?) + añña: other; another; else. (adj.)
attanā: atta ("self", ins. "by, with, through" / abl. "from")
    abl. => "apart from [yourself]"?

piyataro (nom. sing.): "beloved" (from title of sutta)

> “Natthi kho me, mahārāja, kocañño attanā piyataro”.

natthi: "exist" (negated) => "does not exist"
kho me: "to me"
kocañño: koc ("whoever"?) + añño (other)
attanā (ins./abl.): atta
piyataro: "beloved" / "dear"

> Tuyhaṃ pana, mahārāja, atthañño koci attanā piyataro”ti?

tuyhaṃ (acc. case?): tuvaṃ: thou. (nom. sin. of tumha)
atthañño: "is there another?"
    atthu: be it so. (imper. of atthi)
    añña: other; another; else. (adj.)
koci: "whoever"
attanā: atta (abl.)
piyataro: "beloved"

> “Mayhampi kho, mallike, natthañño koci attanā piyataro”ti.

mayhampi: "for me also"
    ahaṃ: I. (nom. sing. of amha)
    pi: "and also"
natthañño: n (not) + atthi (exists) + añño (other / another)
koci: "whoever"
attanā: myself (abl. "from" / "apart from")
piyataro: "beloved"

> Atha kho rājā pasenadi kosalo pāsādā orohitvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami.

- then king pasendadi of kosala descended from the palace

pāsādā - palace
orohitvā - descend
=> yena / tena - pronoun pairs
tenupasaṅkami: ten (tena: "on account of"..."then") + upasaṅkami (approached)
    upasaṅkami: approached. (aor. of upasaṅkamati)

> Upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi.

upasaṅkamitvā: "having approached"
    -tva = "gerund" = "having done X"
bhagavantaṃ:
    bhagavant = ...adj??
    -taṃ = acc.??
abhivādetvā: abhivādeti (caus. of abhivadati) = "having bowed down"
    abhivadati: "speak out" / "call out to" / "greet"
    ???? - how is the causative = bowing down?
ekamantaṃ: ekam (one) + anta (end) = "on one end"
nisīdi: "sat down"

> Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho rājā pasenadi kosalo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca:

- having sat down to one side/end, king pasendati of kosala spoke to the buddha:

> “Idhāhaṃ, bhante, mallikāya deviyā saddhiṃ uparipāsādavaragato

- "I went with queen mallika to the top of the palace"

idhāhaṃ: "I said"??
saddhiṃ: "with"

> mallikaṃ deviṃ etadavocaṃ:

- "...[I said to] queen mallika:"

> ‘atthi nu kho te, mallike, kocañño attanā piyataro’ti?

- "does there exist for you, mallika, another [dearer] than yourself?"

> Evaṃ vutte, mallikā devī maṃ etadavoca:

evaṃ: thus
vutte (pp. of vatti, vac): "spoken"
maṃ (acc.): "to me"

> ‘natthi kho me, mahārāja, kocañño attanā piyataro.

- indeed there does not exist, for me, another [dearer] than myself

> Tuyhaṃ pana, mahārāja, atthañño koci attanā piyataro’ti?

- and you, king, is there another dearer than yourself?

pana: "moreover"
atthañño: atthi (exist) + añño (another)
koci: "some one" / "whoever"

> Evaṃ vutte, ahaṃ, bhante, mallikaṃ deviṃ etadavocaṃ:

- having said this, i, bhante, said to queen mallika:

> ‘mayhampi kho, mallike, natthañño koci attanā piyataro’”ti.

- indeed for me also, mallika, there exists not another someone [dearer] than myself

mayhampi: "for me also"
    ahaṃ: I. (nom. sing. of amha)
    pi: "and also"

> Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi:

atha: then
etamatthaṃ: eta (this) + mattha (brains?)
viditvā (vindati): "having known"
tāyaṃ (loc.): "in [that thing]"?
velāyaṃ (loc.): "at/in that time"
    velā: time; shore; limit; boundary. (f.)
imaṃ: ayam - "this person" / "he"
udānaṃ (nt. nom.?): udāna: an emotional utterance. (nt.)
udānesi (verb. 2nd. sing.): "says something with intense emotion"
    udāneti: utters something with intense emotion. (u + ā + nī + e)

> “Sabbā disā anuparigamma cetasā,

sabba: all
disa: enemy. (m.)
anuparigamma (voc.): "O, above what should be understood"
    upari: above; on; upon; upper; overhead. (ind.)
    gamma: vulgar; what should be understood or attained. (adj.)
cetasā: ???
    case? (many possible)
    meaning?

> nevajjhagā piyataramattanā kvaci.

nevajjhagā: "one would not experience"
    na + eva: not + emphatic = "one would not"
    ajjhagā: came to; obtained; experienced. (pret. of adhigacchati)

piyataramattanā: piyatara (dearest) + m + attana (self)

kvaci: somewhere; in some places. (ind.)

> Evaṃ piyo puthu attā paresaṃ,

evaṃ: thus
piyo: dear
puthu: individual (separate/d)
attā: self
paresaṃ: that other person
    para: other; another; foreign; alien; outsider. (adj.)
    esa: (euphonic form of eso), that person. ()

> Tasmā na hiṃse paramattakāmo”ti.

tasmā: therefore
na: do not
hiṃse (voc.): "O, violence"
    hiṃsā: teasing; injury; hurting. (f.)

paramattakāmo: "the other who is full of self-conceit / self-love" ?
    para: other
    matta: was intoxicated; full of joy; proud of; conceited; polished.
           (- ka), (in cpds.) of the size of; as much as. (pp. of majjati)
    kāma: pleasure; lust; enjoyment; an object of sexual enjoyment. (m.)
