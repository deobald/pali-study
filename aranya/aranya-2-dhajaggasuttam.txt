1. "araññe rukkhamūle vā, suññāgāreva bhikkhavo.

araññe: arañña plural?
rukkhamūle: rukkha (tree) + mūle (root, foundation)
vā: or
suññāgāreva: suññā (void, empty) + gār (house/hut) + eva (even, just)

2. "anussaretha sambuddhaṃ, bhayaṃ tumhāka no siyā.

anussaretha (anussarati): remembers
sambuddhaṃ: the sambuddha
bhayaṃ (bhaya): fear, fright, dread
tumhāka (tumhā): second person ("you")?
    -aka: ?
no siyā: siyā => atthi: exist ...arise?

"remember the sambuddha, then no fear will arise in you"

3. "no ce buddhaṃ sareyyātha, lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

no ce buddhaṃ sareyyātha: if you cannot recall the buddha
    sareyyātha (sarati): remembers
lokajeṭṭhaṃ: loka (world, everyone) + jeṭṭha (elder, supreme)
narāsabhaṃ (narāsabha): world of men

4. "atha dhammaṃ sareyyātha, niyyānikaṃ sudesitaṃ."

atha: then
dhammaṃ: the dhamma
sareyyātha: remember
niyyānikaṃ (niyyānika): leading to salvation / leading out of samsara
sudesitaṃ (sudesita): well-expounded

5. "no ce dhammaṃ sareyyātha, niyyānikaṃ sudesitaṃ."

no ce dhammaṃ sareyyātha: but if you cannot recall the dhamma
niyyānikaṃ sudesitaṃ.: leading out of samsara, well-expounded

6. "atha saṅghaṃ sareyyātha, puññakkhettaṃ anuttaraṃ."

atha: then
saṅghaṃ: the sangha
sareyyātha: remember
puññakkhettaṃ: puñña (wisdom, merit) + kkhettaṃ (???)
anuttaraṃ (anuttara): incomparable, unsurpassed

7. "evaṃ buddhaṃ sarantānaṃ, dhammaṃ saṅghañca bhikkhavo."

evaṃ: this (those)
buddhaṃ: buddha
sarantānaṃ (saranta): remembering
dhammaṃ: dhamma
saṅghañca: sangha
    -añcha = ???
bhikkhavo: bhikkhus

8. "bhayaṃ vā chambhitattaṃ vā, lomahaṃso na hessatī”ti."

bhayaṃ vā: or fear/dread
chambhitattaṃ vā: or to be stupefied (astonish, shock, unable to think properly)
lomahaṃso (lomahaṃsa): "horripilation"
    horripilation = "the erection of hairs on the skin due to cold, fear, or excitement"
na hessatī: not becoming / not producing
    1) bhavati: becoming
    2) janati: to produce, cause
