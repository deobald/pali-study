
Ekapuggalo Vaggo

Navigation: Anguttara Nikaya, (1), M, ekakanipatipali, ekapuggalavaggo

"ekapuggalo, bhikkhave, loke uppajjamāno uppajjati
 bahujanahitāya bahujanasukhāya lokānukampāya atthāya
 hitāya sukhāya devamanussānaṃ.

eka (one) puggalo (person / man)
loke: plural of `loka`? "all the worlds"?
uppajjamāno (uppajjamāna): past tense ("was born")
uppajjati: to be born / to arise
